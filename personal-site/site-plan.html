Overview

Purpose

My company informs bodybuilders and casual gym goers alike of the highest rated supplements and strategies for whatever fitness goal they have in mind. Think, last time you went to the gym, did you know exactly what to be working on? Or did you wander around, overwhelmed by all the options, and then just stick to things you know? This is a problem many beginner gym goers encounter. My company, along with its affordable subscription fee, takes input from experienced bodybuilders, and professionals alike, and informs you of how, when, and what to exercise based on your schedule. As well as the tools and equipment to assist in that process.

Intro

We strive to provide unbiased information on what we do in the fitness industry. Whatever your goal may be, it is worth the effort to get the best, most refined information on what will launch your journey into motion. Our goal is to remove the anxiety and “FOWO” (or Fear Of Working Out). We want to help you to find out what the gym has to offer you.

Advisors

Our site is a central hub for all things gym related. We help you to dive into this lifestyle. We will help you dive into dozens of tips from professional bodybuilders, athletes, and trainers on how to stay motivated and committed to making a change, or improving your experience in the gym. We also provide short histories and accomplishments of our athletic advisors, and how they came to be who they are.

Encouragement

We give you real timelines for reaching a certain goal. In the world today with so much social media clouding around the gym and success stories, it can be confusing or even discouraging when you approach the thought of getting into the gym. You might think that many of the people you see on social media have been working out since their early years when in reality all it takes is diligence and patience.

Guides

We are also committed to showing and educating you on what exercises are most efficient to work and grow the desired muscle group. We go into some of the common misconceptions about exercises and fitness practices in general. Again, with so much social media covering the gym, there are also a shocking amount of misinformed influencers.

Routines

At Gym Guru we also will show you some of the most popular training splits and routines that will work for you. We all have different schedules and motivations for going to the gym, so we will cater to some of the basic schedules that you can try.

Supplements

We will also dive into the different aspects of supplements and the dangers of other more unorthodox chemicals. In this industry, there are hundreds of different high, and low-quality products that have different goals in mind. We at Gym Guru aim to filter out all the companies that have less than desirable goals in mind, and only provide you with the best supplements for you to consider using. We will dive into what is important in pre-workouts and protein powders, and how flavors play into the mix. We also will warn you about the dangers of steroids and how they are perceived on social media today.

Why will people come to your site?

People will choose my site because we offer quality advice on all things gym related, from exercises to supplements to expert advice on why we do what we do. They will also want to visit my site because we store several training plans and splits that would normally require too much time spent switching from site to site to find.

What questions will your visitors expect your site to answer for them?

Why should I go to the gym, what does it add to my life? Where do I start? What will kick off my journey? Would supplements benefit me? If so, where do I start? How do I set goals for myself? How can I stay active and committed? How long will it take me to reach my goals?

What types of activities will visitors want to complete on your site?

They will want to find information on exercises and tips for progress. They will want to dig into proven training plans to inspire them. They will want to look through the interactive layout of muscle groups to dive into.

What information is necessary to complete any actions your site offers?

An email address

Why will your visitors come to your site instead of another to get this information or complete the actions?

They will come to my site because it provides all this information in one central, easy-to-use location. It is cheaper and more direct than other websites and has unbiased opinions on all things gym related.

Audience

The intended audience is new or prospecting gym goers who aren't sure where to start in fitness. Or those who are plateuing in progress and need new ideas. It is also targeting those looking for supplements to support their goals.
